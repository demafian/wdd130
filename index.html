South Africa, Pretoria

Pretoria, one of South Africa’s three capital cities, is the administrative hub known for its jacaranda-lined streets, rich history, and vibrant culture. It features iconic landmarks like the Union Buildings with stunning hilltop views, impressive architecture, and beautiful gardens. The city also boasts numerous parks, including the Pretoria National Botanical Garden, and hosts cultural festivals throughout the year. Combining history, nature, and heritage, Pretoria is a unique destination for both locals and visitors.
